What is an ATS Resume Score?

Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) are software tools used by companies to filter resumes before they reach a human recruiter. An ATS resume score shows how closely your resume matches a specific job description based on keywords, skills, experience signals, and formatting rules.

How This ATS Resume Checker Works

This free ATS resume checker analyzes your resume directly in your browser and compares it with the job description you provide. The score is calculated using keyword match, skills alignment, role relevance, experience indicators, and ATS-friendly formatting. No resumes or personal data are stored.
