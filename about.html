About ATS Resume Checker

ATS Resume Checker is a free online tool designed to help job seekers understand how well their resume matches a specific job description.

Many companies use Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) to automatically screen resumes before they reach a human recruiter. Our tool helps you measure ATS compatibility by analyzing keyword alignment, skills relevance, role matching, experience signals, and formatting factors.

This tool is built for students, freshers, and professionals who want to improve their chances of getting shortlisted for interviews.

Important: Your resume is processed entirely in your browser. We do not store, collect, or share resumes or job descriptions.
